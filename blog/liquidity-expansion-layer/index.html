Unlimited Asset Choices: Alvara’s Liquidity Expansion Layer is Live

Alvara Protocol · February 9, 2026 · 6 min read

TL;DR: With the launch of the Liquidity Expansion Layer, we’ve tremendously expanded the number of assets users can access on our platform, bringing us closer to our ideal: “Thousand assets, infinite BSKTs.”

The Liquidity Expansion Layer is a significant milestone in our story. It opens access to all the assets aggregated by 1inch and makes them available for BSKT creation on Alvara.

The $145 Trillion Opportunity

The global asset management industry is projected to continue growing, driven by investors’ demand for predictable returns without needing to do the work themselves. Hedge funds and others hold $145 Trillion in assets under management, yet what this number doesn’t account for are all those investors who’ll never gain access to their services.

The traditional managed financial services sector is plagued by high inefficiencies while serving only a select few, namely those who’ve reached certain levels of wealth. High minimums keep out millions of people, and as back offices consolidate, the diversity of offerings diminishes.

It’s a pattern we’re deeply familiar with working in an industry that started with a rebellion against bailing out bankers who’d overleveraged themselves at the cost of taxpayers. The system serves the large players, and neglects smaller investors.

The Onchain Gap

In a choppy market, discretionary strategies have been one of the biggest winners. That means, the highest returns were often achieved by those actively managing their portfolio rather than creating it once and HODLing.

Looking at the current onchain market for such products, the majority of protocols are better described as risk curators. This means, they create vaults (smart contracts that’ll execute their strategy) which investors can lock funds in in return for LP tokens which they can then further use in DeFi.

Yet we’re still nowhere near the trillions in AUM of TradFi, with a generous $35 billion in onchain assets managed in 2025.

It’s also worth mentioning that these risk curators have a limited range of vaults available (for quality sake), yet do not allow anyone to create their own.

That’s the gap we’re filling with Alvara and baskets (BSKTs). With Alvara, anyone can become an asset manager or invest in others’ BSKTs. This is made possible by our custom token standard, which ensures that BSKTs remain composable, 100% backed, and transferable. You can read more about the standard here.

With full transparency ensured, any BSKT creators’ performance is tracked, providing a fully meritocratic playing field, where the best rise to the top of the leaderboard.

Yet for BSKTers to do their best, what they needed was access to more assets.
