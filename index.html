The Standard

The ERC-7621 Basket Token Standard

A new token standard submitted as an Ethereum Improvement Proposal (EIP) that enables the creation, management, and trading of multi-asset onchain baskets. ERC-7621 transforms how we build and interact with onchain portfolios.

Basket Token Standard

Create customizable crypto baskets containing any combination of ERC-20 assets. Configure weights, management parameters, and fee structures at deployment.

Fungible LP Tokens

Each BSKT is an ERC-7621 token built on ERC-721, holding an unlimited number of ERC-20 assets. Investors receive ERC-20 LP shares — tradeable on any DEX and compatible with existing DeFi infrastructure.

Automated Fee Distribution

Built-in management fee mechanisms execute at the protocol level. Managers receive a configurable percentage of TVL - no off-chain payment infrastructure needed.

Permissionless Deployment

Anyone can create a basket and become a fund manager. No gatekeepers, no approval process, no minimum capital requirements.

Onchain Rebalancing

Adjust tokens and weights within a single transaction. Add or remove underlying assets and modify portfolio composition in real time.

Transferable Ownership

ERC-20 LP shares and management NFTs are fully transferable onchain. Broader marketplace support for management-rights transfers is planned.

Governance

Gauge Voting

veALVA holders nominate balance to a chosen BSKT every 7 days, directing the ALVA reward flow.

Deflationary Mechanism

Include 5% or more ALVA in your BSKT to unlock lower protocol fees. ALVA is purchased from the open market - creating sustained buy pressure and removing supply from circulation.
